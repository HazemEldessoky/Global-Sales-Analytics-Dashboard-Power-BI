# 📌 Overview

Businesses generate massive amounts of sales data every day, making it difficult to monitor performance, identify trends, and make informed decisions.

This project presents a complete **Global Sales Analytics Dashboard** developed in **Power BI** that transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights. The dashboard enables stakeholders to analyze sales performance, monitor profitability, evaluate product performance, understand customer behavior, and explore sales distribution across different countries.

Developed as part of my **Power BI Capstone Project** during my Data Analytics journey with EraaSoft, this solution demonstrates the complete Business Intelligence workflow—from data preparation and modeling to interactive dashboard development.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Business leaders often struggle to answer important questions such as:

- Which countries generate the highest revenue?
- Which products contribute the most to profitability?
- How are sales changing over time?
- Who are the most valuable customers?
- Which product categories drive business growth?
- How does customer purchasing behavior differ across segments?

Without interactive reporting, answering these questions requires significant manual effort and delays strategic decision-making.

This dashboard centralizes all business metrics into one interactive reporting solution.

# 💡 Key Insights

The dashboard provides several valuable business insights, including:

- Revenue and profit steadily increased over the analysis period, indicating positive business growth.
- A small number of products generated a significant share of total revenue, highlighting the importance of top-performing products.
- Return rates varied across products, helping identify items that may require quality improvements or further investigation.
- Sales distribution revealed clear differences in performance across countries, allowing businesses to identify high-potential markets.
- Customer segmentation by occupation and income level uncovered purchasing patterns that can support more targeted marketing strategies.
- Revenue per customer trends helped evaluate customer value over time.
- Interactive analysis enables users to drill into specific products, customers, or regions for deeper business understanding.

---

# 🚀 Business Recommendations

Based on the dashboard insights, the following recommendations can help improve business performance:

- Focus marketing and sales efforts on high-performing countries to maximize revenue growth.
- Increase inventory and promotional activities for best-selling products.
- Investigate products with higher return rates to improve quality and customer satisfaction.
- Develop customer retention strategies for high-value customer segments.
- Use customer demographics and purchasing behavior to create personalized marketing campaigns.
- Continuously monitor KPIs and sales trends to support proactive business decisions.
- Expand the use of interactive reporting across departments to improve business visibility.
